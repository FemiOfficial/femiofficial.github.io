About



Hello there, I am Alayesanmi Femi, CEO BrytHub. I am a friendly and very receptive programmer. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and Java are the tools of my craft. I am familiar with responsive design techniques, approaching all projects with a mobile-first view, to ensure excellent user experience on every project i am opportuned to handle.

At BrytHub, we love programming and utilizing our skill in the craft to create wonderful websites and mobile apps, and that's our motivation to constantly improve out manpower on various aspects of our craft. You could reasonably describe BrytHub as a software development consulting firm because we do both the User Experience Design, User Interface Design, Front-End Development, Back-End Development, System Administration of live applications thereby making ideas a reality with live applications.

We keep up-to-date on the latest developments within the Web and mobile development Industry, taking new ways of working and the use of frameworks into our development repotoire. Our team of developers are specialised in website and mobile apps development, creating responsive web applications suited to every type of device.

We also try to guide our clients through the early stages of projects which include planning, requirement gathering and research, to help clients have a robust understanding and fore-knowledege of the intended system and then design your website/mobile app, and finally build the entire site for our clients.
